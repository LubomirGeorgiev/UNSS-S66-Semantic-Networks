Definition
  • [noun]The capacity to have an effect on the character, development, or behaviour of someone or something, or the effect itself:
  • [noun]The capacity or power of persons or things to be a compelling force on or produce effects on the actions, behavior, opinions, etc., of others:
  • [noun]A person or thing with the capacity to have an influence on someone or something:
